Provide a fit note

If you need to give the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) a fit note, you can send it to them online.

You can either:

Let the DWP get it directly from the NHS

By providing some details your fit note can be accessed from the NHS systems. You’ll be asked for your permission first.

Use your mobile phone to scan your paper fit note

Use the camera on your mobile phone to scan the barcode on your paper fit note. You’ll see your digital fit note, then can send it instantly.

Enter the unique code from your fit note

Fill in your details and the unique code from your paper fit note to see your digital fit note - then send it instantly.

Post your fit note

You can post your fit note to:

12 Barrow Home
London
W5 7JT

Don’t post your fit note unless you have to - it’s cheaper, quicker and safer to send it online.
